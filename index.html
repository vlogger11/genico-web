Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most from our customers.

What products does Genico Tech Solutions supply? +
We are a Pune-based trader and supplier of industrial automation products — including Mitsubishi VFDs, Servo Motors and Servo Drives, Delta VFDs, Mitsubishi & Delta PLCs, Delta & Mitsubishi HMIs, SMPS power supplies, Siemens servo motors and Phoenix Contact FL Switches.
Are the products you supply genuine and brand-new? +
Yes. Every product is carefully verified for authenticity, performance and compliance with industry standards before it reaches you, so you can rely on dependable operation in demanding industrial applications.
Can you help me choose the right product for my application? +
Absolutely. Our team offers technical assistance to help you match the ideal automation components to your operational needs. Share your requirements via our contact form or call us, and we will guide you to the best fit.
What payment and delivery options do you offer? +
We accept online payments (NEFT / RTGS / IMPS), Cheque / DD and Cash. Goods are dispatched by road through our well-organised logistics network to ensure timely, reliable delivery.
Do you supply outside Pune and Maharashtra? +
Yes. While we are based in Pune, our road-transport logistics allow us to serve industrial customers across the region. Contact us with your location and we will confirm delivery details.
How do I request a price quote? +
Browse our products and click Send Inquiry on any product, or use the Contact page to send us the model and quantity you need. We respond promptly with a competitive quote.
